Recipes for BANGA Soup

BANGA Soup is a remarkably a delicious meal prepare in different variety by tribes and people of Nigeria.

Ingredients:

500g assorted meats, 250g dried and smoked fish, 255g bushmeat, 1 palm soup base, 225g ground dried prawns, 600ml beef stock or water, 225 okra,sliced into thin rings 60g tomato puree, 2 hot chillies(eg. Scotch bonnet), pounded to a paste 100g igbo(ground-pepper) or use watercress, 1 onion, sliced 1/2 tsp ataieko(alligator pepper), 1/2 tsp ashanti pepper, ground salt to taste

METHOD

  1. Place the washed meat in a large pot, add a drop of water or stock season with salt and ground pepper and boil for 30 minutes or until tender
  2. Add the smoked fish and stockfish, cook for another 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare the oil-palm nut to extract the oil by boiling the washed nuts for 20 minutes until soft.
  4. Remove from water and pound to remove the oil
  5. Pass through a sieve to separate the kernels from the chaff.
  6. Pour the strained pulp into the meat together with the sliced peppers onions' tomatoes puree and Okra
  7. Sprinkle in the crayfish and cook for 15 minutes until the soup is farily reduced and thickened to coat the back of spoon
  8. Check seasoning and serve with pounded yam(Iyan) or Usin/Egun Obobo(starch or plantain pudding)
